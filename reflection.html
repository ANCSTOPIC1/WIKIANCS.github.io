Chia Zheng Yang

Throughout this WIKI BLOG assignment, I was able to learn more in-depth on how a gyroscope is used in the aviation industry. The creation of gyroscopes has really widened the navigation and flight assistant capabilities of the aircraft's flight instruments to what we have today in the modernised generation.

What has actually gotten me more interested in the design of the gyroscope is on how it is able to derive the position of the plane just by using a few gimbals and rings. I am glad to have contributed to this WIKI BLOG which has allowed me to understand the use of a gyroscope better.

Gavin Tan

Through this project, I was able to discover that there are many different types of gyroscope each with its own pros and cons. It led me to further research on the theory and application of the fundamentals of gyroscopic precession. Concepts such as fibre optic gyroscopes sound absurd and impossible but are widely used in aviation. It is undeniable that gyroscope technology is still at its infancy with MEMS gyroscope still developing for aviation use. It could be a huge game changer once breakthrough for MEMS technology is realized. This effectively brings down fuel cost and increased storage capacity.

Apart from learning about gyroscopes through this project. I was also able to take this opportunity to learn about MEMS gyroscope for practical usages in personal projects such as integrating it together with an arduino board as well. It was a rather interesting side project to work on, which taught me quite a lot on the working principles of gyroscopes.
